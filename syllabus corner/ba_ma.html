About Bachelor of Arts(BA)


Bachelor of Arts in Education or B.A Education is a three-year undergraduate degree programme where the learners are educated in the domain of education and its applications. Students who have a knack for teaching are the ones who usually opt for the B.A Education programme. It is not mandatory for the B.A Education aspirants to pass their 10+2 level qualifying exam from a particular stream. Students from Science stream are taught how to teach science subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and Biology and so on.
