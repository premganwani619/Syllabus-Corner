CA Foundation Question Paper Pattern

The CA foundation Exam Pattern is mentioned hereunder:- CA Foundation examination contains four papers.

  1. Paper 1 and 2 papers are of subjective types.
    • Principles And Practice Of Accounting
    • Business Laws And Business Correspondence 
  2. Paper 3 and 4 are of objective types. 
    • Business Mathematics,Logical Reasoning, And Statistics
    • Business Economics And Business & Commercial Knowledge
  3. The whole CA Foundation examination is of 400 marks and every paper is for 100 marks respectively.
  4. Negative marking is also applied to objective papers where any incorrect answer will subtract 1/4th mark in the paper.
  5. The exam is taken into two mediums- English and Hindi, except Section B of Paper 2. It is compulsory that this section of the question paper is answered in English only.

Passing Marks for CA Foundation Examination

After appearing in the CA Foundation Exam, the students should get:-

  1. At least 40% marks in each of the papers.
  2. At least 50% marks in total of all the papers, after deducting the negative marking.

    Frequently Asked Questions About CA Foundation Question Papers 2021 

    1. How theoretical questions are answered in the CA Foundation Papers?

    Ans:-  Two theory papers are there in the CA Foundation exam paper are Principles and Practices of Accounting and Business Laws and Business Correspondence and Reporting. The answers must be specific and to the point. Dont miss out on any important points otherwise, your marks will be deducted. Try to explain the exact point with an accurate answer and dont fill the answer sheet with mince words.

    2. In CA Foundation Accounts Paper, there is one option where we have to answer 5 questions out of 6. What will happen if I answer all 6 questions?

    Ans:- According to the ICAI rule, if you answer all 6 questions where you have to answer only 5 questions then the marks of the first 5 answers will be evaluated while the 6th answer will be left unreviewed.

    3. How to solve CA Foundation Paper and crack the examination easily?

    Ans:- First, try to finish your syllabus as fast as you can for every subject. Then, solve all the CA foundation sample papers, mock test papers, revision test papers by managing your time. Solve the CA question paper in a precise way and with to-the-point answers. By practicing the above-mentioned things you surely crack the examination easily.
